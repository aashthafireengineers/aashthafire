Fire Safety Certificate (FSC)/ Fire NOC

An official document issued by the fire safety authorities in India, the Fire Safety Certificate (FSC) acts as a No Objection Certificate (NOC), that permits a building or premise to operate legally and acknowledges that it is compliant with relevant fire safety regulations.

  • It ensures that the fire safety system incorporated in the building or premise is complying with the current fire safety legislation
  • For coverage on commercial or industrial structures, many insurance companies demand a valid FSC
  • Most importantly it is essential to provide maximum safety for people and property

What are the steps involved in providing the Fire Safety Certificate (FSC)/ Fire NOC?

  • Online submission of a duly filled application for obtaining the FSC, along with the details of the fire safety systems incorporated in the premise
  • Inspection will be conducted by the fire department to ensure compliance with the safety standards and current regulations
  • Issuance of the Fire Safety Certificate by the local fire department
